Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total:Approximately 10 minutes

  • Preparation:5 minutes

  • Cooking:5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs

  • Salt, to taste

  • epper, to teste

  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil

  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables

Instructions

  1. 1.Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a fork until smooth. Optionally, add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.

  2. 2.Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.

  3. 3.Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted, pour the beaten eggs into the pan and let them cook undisturbed for 1-2 minutes, until the edges start to set.

  4. 4.Add fillings (optional): When the eggs begin to set but are still slightly runny on top, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette.

  5. 5.Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook, use a spatula to fold it in half. Cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate.

  6. 6.Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper to taste.
